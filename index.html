Privacy Policy

Crystal Cascade  ·  by chAs

Last updated: May 2026

📋 Overview

Crystal Cascade ("the app", "we", "our") is developed and operated by chAs Tech Group. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights as a user.

We do not require you to create an account. We do not collect your name, email, or any personally identifiable information directly. Your game progress is stored locally on your device only.

📦 Information We Collect

We collect the following categories of data, primarily through third-party SDKs:

  • Advertising ID (GAID) — Used by Google AdMob to serve relevant ads. You can reset or opt out of this via your device settings.
  • Device information — Device model, OS version, screen size, language. Used for ad targeting and crash diagnostics.
  • App usage data — Which levels you play, session length, in-app events. Used to improve gameplay and ad relevance.
  • Purchase data — If you make an in-app purchase, Google Play processes the transaction. We receive confirmation only (not your payment details).
  • Crash reports — Anonymous crash data to help us fix bugs.

We do not collect: real name, email address, phone number, location, contacts, photos, or microphone data.

💾 Local Data Storage

Your game progress — including level completion, coins, hints, lives, and settings — is stored locally on your device using Android SharedPreferences. This data never leaves your device and is not linked to any account.

Uninstalling the app permanently deletes all locally stored data.

🔗 Third-Party Services

Crystal Cascade uses the following third-party services, each with their own privacy policies:

  • Google AdMob — Displays advertisements. May collect device identifiers and usage data.
    Google Privacy Policy ↗
  • Google Play Billing — Processes in-app purchases securely.
    Google Play Policies ↗
  • Flutter Local Notifications — Sends push notifications to your device. No data is transmitted to external servers.
We do not sell, trade, or rent your data to any third parties. Data shared with SDKs is governed by those services' own privacy policies.

📢 Advertising

Crystal Cascade is free to play and supported by ads served through Google AdMob. Ads may be personalised based on your Advertising ID and usage patterns.

You can opt out of personalised advertising at any time:

  • Android: Settings → Google → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalisation
  • You can also reset your Advertising ID from the same menu

Purchasing a Remove Ads pass in the shop stops all ad display in the app for the duration of your pass.

🔔 Push Notifications

Crystal Cascade may send local push notifications to remind you about daily bonuses, life regeneration, and limited-time offers. These notifications are generated entirely on your device — no data is sent to our servers.

You can disable notifications at any time via Settings → Apps → Crystal Cascade → Notifications on your device.

👶 Children's Privacy (COPPA)

Crystal Cascade is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided personal information, please contact us and we will take steps to delete such information promptly.

As Crystal Cascade contains advertising, we do not designate it as a children's app and it is rated for general audiences (13+).

🇪🇺 Your Rights (GDPR & CCPA)

If you are located in the European Economic Area or California, you have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction or deletion of your data
  • Opt out of personalised advertising
  • Lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority

Since most data is stored locally on your device, you can delete it at any time by uninstalling the app. For data held by third-party SDKs (e.g. AdMob), please refer to Google's privacy controls.

🔒 Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information. Game data stored on your device is protected by Android's application sandboxing. In-app purchases are handled entirely by Google Play's secure billing infrastructure — we never see or store your payment details.

📝 Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this page periodically. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
